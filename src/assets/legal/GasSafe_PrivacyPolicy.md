# Gas Safe Register – Privacy Policy & Regulatory Disclosure

**Last Updated:** 1 March 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This document supplements the main GasPilot Privacy Policy and specifically addresses the collection, processing, and potential disclosure of personal data in connection with Gas Safe Register compliance, CP12 certificate generation, and regulatory obligations under UK law.

GasPilot ("the App") is operated by the App Developer ("we", "us", "our"). This policy applies to all users who enter a Gas Safe registration number and generate CP12 certificates through the App ("Engineers").

---

## 2. Data We Collect in Connection with Gas Safe Compliance

In addition to the data described in our main Privacy Policy, we collect and store the following data specifically related to Gas Safe compliance:

| Data Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Registration Details** | Gas Safe registration number (6 digits), Gas Safe ID card number (7 digits), ACS qualification details |
| **Profile Information** | Full name, email address, phone number, business name and address |
| **Certificate Records** | All CP12 certificates generated, including property addresses, landlord/tenant details, appliance inspection data, inspection dates, and digital signatures |
| **Consent Records** | Timestamp and record of the Engineer's acceptance of the Gas Safe Terms of Service |
| **Technical Data** | IP address, device identifiers, session logs, timestamps of all actions taken within the App |
| **Email Delivery Data** | Recipient email addresses, email subject lines, and PDF certificate attachments when documents are sent via the App's email feature (processed by Resend, our transactional email provider) |

---

## 3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process the above data on the following lawful bases under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR):

- **Contractual necessity** (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary for the performance of our contract with the Engineer (i.e., providing the App and its certificate generation functionality).
- **Legitimate interests** (Article 6(1)(f)): We have a legitimate interest in maintaining the integrity and lawful use of our platform, preventing fraud, and cooperating with regulators.
- **Legal obligation** (Article 6(1)(c)): We may be required to process and disclose data to comply with legal obligations, including responding to lawful requests from regulators and law enforcement.

---

## 4. Regulatory Disclosure – Mandatory Sharing Without Prior Consent

**The Engineer acknowledges and agrees that we reserve the right to disclose the following data directly to the Gas Safe Register, the Health and Safety Executive (HSE), Capita Gas Registration and Ancillary Services Ltd, Trading Standards, the Police, or any other competent regulatory or law enforcement authority, without obtaining the Engineer's prior consent:**

- Full profile information (name, email, phone, business details);
- Gas Safe registration number and ID card number;
- IP address and device identifiers associated with the Engineer's account;
- Complete records of all CP12 certificates generated through the App, including PDF copies;
- Timestamps and audit logs of all relevant account activity;
- Consent records (acceptance of Gas Safe Terms of Service).

**This disclosure may occur in the following circumstances:**

1. We receive a **formal or informal request, notice, or enquiry** from the Gas Safe Register, HSE, Capita, or law enforcement regarding the Engineer or their use of the App;
2. We have **reasonable grounds to suspect** that the Engineer has entered a false or misappropriated Gas Safe registration number;
3. We have **reasonable grounds to suspect** that the Engineer is generating fraudulent certificates, performing gas work without valid registration, or otherwise acting unlawfully;
4. An **investigation into illegal gas work**, unregistered gas work, or trademark infringement involving the Gas Safe Register brand is initiated by any competent authority;
5. We are **required by law** to make such a disclosure.

We will not be required to notify the Engineer before or after such disclosure unless required to do so by law.

**Email Delivery Sub-processor:** When CP12 certificates or other documents are sent via email through the App, email delivery is handled by **Resend** (resend.com), a third-party transactional email service acting as our data sub-processor. Resend processes recipient email addresses, email content, and PDF attachments solely for the purpose of delivering the email. Certificate data transmitted through Resend may form part of any regulatory disclosure described above.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain all CP12 certificate records, audit logs, and consent records for a minimum of **six (6) years** from the date of creation, in line with standard UK limitation periods and the Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998 requirement for landlords to retain records for two years (we retain longer for our own legal protection).

Gas Safe registration details and consent timestamps are retained for the lifetime of the account and for six (6) years following account deletion.

## 6. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you retain the right to:

- **Access** your personal data;
- **Rectify** inaccurate personal data;
- **Erase** personal data (subject to our legal retention obligations);
- **Restrict** processing in certain circumstances;
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests;

**Please note:** Where data is retained for regulatory compliance or legal defence purposes, we may be unable to fulfil erasure or restriction requests until the applicable retention period has expired.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **support@gaspilot.app**
